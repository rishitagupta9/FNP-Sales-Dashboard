FNP Sales Analysis Dashboard (Excel Project)

The FNP Sales Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Excel project built to analyze sales performance across multiple business dimensions such as:

1.Monthly revenue trends

2.Product categories

3.Customer occasions

4.Order volume

5.Revenue by time

The goal of this project was to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights using Excel.

 Overall Performance Snapshot:

Total Revenue: ₹3.52M

Total Orders: 1,000

Average Order Value: ₹3,520.98

Average Delivery Time: 5.53 days

These KPIs provide a clear overview of company performance.

 Revenue Insights:
 
 Monthly Revenue Trend:

Peak months: February & August

Lower performance observed during May–July

Sales spikes driven by seasonal occasions (Valentine’s Day, Raksha Bandhan)

 Revenue by Category:

Colors – Top performing category

Soft Toys – Strong emotional gifting demand

Sweets – Consistent contribution

Customer behavior indicates preference for occasion-based and emotion-driven products.

 Revenue by Occasion:

Anniversary

Raksha Bandhan

Holi

All Occasions

Cultural and personal celebrations significantly influence sales.

 Product Performance:
 
 Top Products by Revenue:

High-performing products generated consistent premium revenue, indicating strong demand for gift sets.

 Top Products by Order Volume

Mid-range products showed higher order frequency, suggesting affordability drives volume.

 Customer Behavior Insights-:
  Revenue by Hour:

Peak purchase window: 4 PM – 9 PM

Lowest activity: Early morning hours

This suggests that evening marketing campaigns may improve conversions.

 Business Recommendations

Increase stock during February & August

Promote high-performing categories

Launch campaigns during evening peak hours

Introduce mid-year promotional strategies

Optimize delivery time for better customer satisfaction
